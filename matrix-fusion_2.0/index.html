About the event

We are thrilled to announce the upcoming one-day technical event, Matrix Fusion, organized by the AI ML Department in association with Yen Artificia. Scheduled to be held every even semester, Matrix Fusion promises to be an immersive experience into the realms of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
This event aims to foster a collaborative environment where enthusiasts, professionals, and students converge to explore the latest trends, advancements, and applications in AI and ML. With insightful talks from industry leaders, hands-on workshops, and engaging panel discussions, Matrix Fusion strives to ignite innovative ideas and cultivate a deeper understanding of these cutting-edge technologies.

About YEN-ARTIFICIA


Yen-Artificia, the esteemed association of the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning,YIT is dedicated to fostering innovation, learning, and collaboration among students and enthusiasts. Each semester, Yen-Artificia hosts a series of dynamic and engaging events designed to challenge participants, enhance their skills, and ignite their passion for AI and machine learning.
