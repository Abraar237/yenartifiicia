About Hackathon

UNSCRAMBLE-2.0 is the National level Hackathon organized by Yen-Artificia,Department of AIML,YIT which encourages college students to showcase their creativity and problem-solving skills. It is an in-person 24-hour event for innovatively fostering real-world problem-solving skills.

Participants can fully engage with problem statements tailored to resonate with their individual interests and aspirations. This inclusivity encourages the incorporation of diverse perspectives, fostering a collaborative and dynamic approach to problem-solving.

UNSCRAMBLE-2.0 ,2024 is open to all college students in India!

  • Date: 24th JAN to 25th JAN 2024

  • Team Size: 3 members

  • Participation fee of Rs. 500 to be paid after getting shortlisted
